# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

AgentisOrchestra is built by a small team in Quebec City. We want this community to be welcoming to everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Standards

**Expected:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on the technical merits of ideas
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Help newcomers get started

**Not acceptable:**
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to alex@agentislab.ai. We will review and respond within 72 hours.
